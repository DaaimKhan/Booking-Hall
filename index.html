The Founder : Haji Ajmal Khan

Haji Ajmal embarked on a remarkable journey in 2014, starting on the auspicious day of 26th January. Despite the challenges of a demanding private duty, he dedicated himself to building the Al-Wali Marriage Hall. His determination and hard work paid off, and the hall was completed in 2017.

His passion and commitment go beyond construction. He has always believed in giving back to the community. The Al-Wali Marriage Hall is a testament to this belief, as it offers top-notch marriage and event arrangements at incredibly affordable prices.

His journey is an inspiration. Not only did he build a magnificent hall, but he also reached out to help the less fortunate. Al-Wali Marriage Hall is a place where dreams come true for those who could only dream of affording a beautiful venue for their special occasions.
